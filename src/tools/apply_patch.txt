Edit files via a simplified diff format.

Format:
*** Begin Patch
*** Add File: path/to/file.ts
+ new line

*** Update File: path/to/existing.ts
@@ context line
- old line
+ new line
*** End of File

*** Move to: path/to/new_location.ts
*** Delete File: path/to/obsolete.ts
*** End Patch

Usage:
- Safe for multi-file structural changes.
- Ensure context lines in 'Update File' are accurate.
